History

Exciting Expeditions began not as a company, but as a passion project. In the summer of 1972, two college friends and avid kayakers, Mark and Jenna, found themselves guiding their less-experienced friends down the local river on large, inflatable military surplus rafts. They saw the sheer joy and awe on their friends' faces and recognized an opportunity.

The following year, they officially founded "White Water Rafting Company," operating out of a converted garage with a single raft, a beat-up pickup truck, and a hand-painted sign by the river's main access point. Their business model was simple: a small fee for a wild ride and a promise of safety. Word quickly spread about the thrilling adventures offered by Mark and Jenna. By their third season, they had expanded to three rafts and hired two additional guides. What started as a passion project soon grew into a thriving business.
